Brief History of the Company

Noel Infrastructures Pvt Ltd

Since 1990, NOEL Group has been a leader in traffic infrastructure, energy, and media, completing landmark projects in Chennai, Bangalore, Madurai, Trichy, Coimbatore, Kolkata, and Mumbai. A pioneer under the JNNSM scheme, NOEL executed India's first 1MW solar plant in 2011.

Read more →

ARBT Building Products Pvt Ltd

Since 2011, ARBT has specialized in manufacturing and installing building facades, aluminum doors, and windows for global brands from France, Germany, Japan, and beyond, delivering projects across India including airports and commercial buildings.

Read more →

Dynamic SS Engg & Constrn (P) Ltd

Established in 2004, Dynamic SS excels in fabricating and erecting large-diameter tanks, vessels, piping, and structural components for power plants, refineries, cement and steel plants, and irrigation projects.
